About Me

Welcome to my Portfolio! My name is Mikaela and on this web page you will learn a little bit about my interests, and most importantly my work. Lets start with my education! I love to learn about various topics and my interests are so diverse that it has provided me the opportunity to gain experience and exposure to what I really want do in the future.

The things I enjoy most in life are spending time with my loved ones, dancing, reading, traveling, and learning new things. I absolutely love all things that are both creative and logical! Art, Music, Dance, Design, etc! There is so much inspiration in the world and being able to use that platform as a developer is so exciting!
